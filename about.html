About us

Welcome to DoBu Martial Arts, where passion, discipline, and dedication come together to create a unique training experience for every member. Located in a state-of-the-art facility, we offer a range of classes across various martial arts disciplines, fitness training, and self-defense courses. Our gym is designed to cater to everyone, from beginners looking to learn the basics to seasoned athletes striving to perfect their skills. At DoBu Martial Arts, we believe in creating a supportive and motivating environment that helps our members unlock their full potential, both physically and mentally.

Our facilities include a large, matted martial arts training area, a fully-equipped gym with top-of-the-line equipment, a relaxing sauna, a rejuvenating steam room, and spacious changing and shower facilities. With experienced instructors, a friendly community, and flexible class schedules, DoBu Martial Arts is here to help you meet your fitness and self-defense goals.

Our history

DoBu Martial Arts was born out of a deep passion for martial arts and a vision to create a community where people of all ages and backgrounds can discover the power of discipline and resilience. Our founder, a lifelong martial artist, recognized the need for a space that would not only offer top-quality training but also foster a welcoming, inclusive environment where members could grow, connect, and challenge themselves.

Since our establishment, DoBu Martial Arts has grown into a family of dedicated instructors, passionate students, and supportive members. We’re committed to empowering our community with the confidence, strength, and skills they need to thrive in everyday life. Whether you’re here to get fit, learn self-defense, or pursue martial arts as a lifelong journey, we’re excited to be a part of your story.
